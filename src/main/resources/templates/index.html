About us

E-Agriculture is an innovative online platform designed to empower farmers with essential information for optimized crop selection and planning. With our advanced technologies, including the integration of ESP32, we provide farmers with accurate recommendations based on their chosen location on the map, historical data analysis, and detailed information about recommended crops.

Our user-friendly interface ensures that farmers can make informed decisions to maximize their agricultural productivity.
